Introducing qipo


Qipo /Kee-poh/ n:

A "bring your own app" collaboration network which lets you stop worrying about tools and start working with people.

Make External Collaboration Easy
We still rely on email because everyone can use it. Companies which depend on close client, contractor, or partner relationships can't take full advantage of modern collaboration tools because competing options are incompatible.

Cross-Platform Productivity Plumbing
Plug your productivity tools into Qipo and we'll connect them with the companies you work with. Qipo translates between apps so you don't need to worry about it. Your chats, calendars, tasks, and files are now universally compatible.


Sync the Essentials

Qipo meets you where you're at: stick with your usual tools, we'll translate to everyone else's.
